About Us

The Special Friends Network is a grassroots initiative that was started to bring together people of all abilities. We are a social group that focuses on connecting and celebrating our neurodiverse world. Inclusion isn’t simply an idea, it’s our Motto.

We are currently located and operating in Halton Region and welcome Members from Halton and surrounding areas. Life skills building, confidence, independence, physical wellness and friendship are all important components when developing each event.
